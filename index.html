Massimo Ostuzzi

Since October 2023, I am a Ph.D. student at Ruhr University Bochum, under the supervision of Alex May and Michael Walter. My research focus is {classical, quantum} public-key cryptanalysis.

I’m part of the EU project Marie Curie Doctoral Network QSI (Quantum Safe Internet).

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics in 2021 from University of Padova, under the supervision of Matteo Longo. In 2023, I was awarded with a Master's degree in Mathematics from University of Padova, under the supervision of Alessio Caminata and Alberto Tonolo.
